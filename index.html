Word Cloud

This word cloud visualizes the most frequently mentioned terms in reported Bigfoot sightings.

Key findings indicate that 'forest', 'night', and 'large' are commonly associated with sightings, suggesting typical conditions or perceptions during encounters.

Bar Chart Race

This dynamic bar chart race shows the changing frequencies of sightings over time across different states.

The race highlights spikes in sightings which may correlate with media events or changes in public interest.

Line Chart Witness Count

This line chart tracks the number of witnesses per sighting, providing insights into the social aspects of these reports.

Most sightings are reported by individuals or small groups, which may affect the reliability of the reports.

Bubble Map

This geographic visualization maps the density of sightings using bubbles, scaled by the number of reports per location.

High-density areas may indicate regions with greater public awareness or actual clusters of activity.

Scatterplot

This scatterplot differentiates sightings by confusion and supernatural belief, using color and shape coding respectively.

There appears to be no strong correlation between the level of confusion and supernatural belief in these sightings.
